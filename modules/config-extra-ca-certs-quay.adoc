= Adding additional Certificate Authorities for {productname}

[role="_abstract"]
Certificate Authorities (CAs) enable {productname} to verify SSL/TLS connections to external services such as OIDC providers, LDAP servers, and storage backends. The steps for adding additional CAs differ between standalone deployments and {productname-ocp}.
