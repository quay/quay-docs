= Prerequisites

[role="_abstract"]
Before you deploy a proof of concept {productname} registry, ensure your host meets minimum hardware requirements and you have {rhel} 9 with an active subscription. You also need Podman for running containers in this guide.

ifeval::["{productname}" == "Red Hat Quay"]
* Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) 9
** Obtain the latest version of {rhel} 9 from the Red Hat Customer Portal.
** See the {rhel} 9 product documentation for installation instructions.
* An active subscription to Red Hat
endif::[]
* Two or more virtual CPUs
* 4 GB or more of RAM
* Approximately 30 GB of disk space on your test system, which can be broken down as follows:
** Approximately 10 GB of disk space for the {rhel} operating system.
** Approximately 10 GB of disk space for Docker storage for running three containers.
** Approximately 10 GB of disk space for {productname} local storage.
+
[NOTE]
====
CEPH or other local storage might require more memory.
====
+
See the Quay 3.x sizing guidelines for more information on sizing.
* The following architectures are supported for {productname}:
** amd64/x86_64
** s390x
** ppc64le
